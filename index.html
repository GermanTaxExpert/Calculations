Calculation fees ruling Germany

The German tax authorities generally levy a fee for the issuance of a ruling. The fee depends on the amount of taxes at stake (fee base) and is calculated based on section 89 of the German General Tax Code in connection with sections 34 and 39 subsec. 2 of the German Court Costs Law. If the fee base is lower than EUR 10,000, no fee is levied.
